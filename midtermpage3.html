Events and Activities

This Halloween year, our goal is to bring you memorable, festive and fun activities(21+). Refer below to find more information about the different events we have to offer. It's everything you ever wanted in a party.

Previous Next
'Most Ghoulish' Costume Competition
Wanna really get into the spirit of Hallowen? Bring your best costume to be judged by a panel! Not only is it the highlight of the night, but winner recieves a free bottle of any liquor of their choice, a $100 giftcard, and crown and sash for their victory! (Please note that in order to have admission to the competition, you must purchase the V.I.P ticket at checkout!)
'Escape Room from Hell'
What's a good halloween without jumps and scares? This is not for the weak of heart. If you're up to it, test your courage in our Escape Room from Hell. In a party of 6, use your knowledge to get out alive from the tormented beings that seek blood and revenge.
Festive Open Bar and DJ
You know what makes a great night? Great drinks and music! Come discover the amazing drink menu we have prepared. Not only that, but have a live DJ who will be playing all types of genre, and even taking requests all night!
Pumpkin Smashing
Carving pumpkins is for kids. Let's get crazy and smash some pumpkins!
